Hafren

Exploring boundaries, crossing borders

Croeso / Welcome

Afon Hafren (River Severn). This ancient border between Wales and England, a place where the spiritual and mortal worlds meet, now lends its name to the creative world of guitarist-composer Chris Roberts. Hafren is an interdisciplinary artistic universe that explores extremes of genre and crossovers between artforms.
